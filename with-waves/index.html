FizzBuzz with Sine Waves

sin(πn/3) is zero exactly when n is a multiple of 3, and sin(πn/5) when n is a multiple of 5. (Not sin(2πn/3) — a wave with period 3 also crosses zero at 1.5, 4.5, … between the ticks. Halving the frequency leaves zeros only on the multiples.) Fizz when the first wave crosses zero, Buzz when the second does, FizzBuzz when both do.
