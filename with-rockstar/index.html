Why these lyrics are a program

Midnight takes your heart and your soul…
declares a function Midnight(heart, soul). The next two lines are its body: while heart ≥ soul, subtract (without) — modulo by repeated subtraction, so the lyrics never have to say the unpoetic word "modulo".
Desire is a lovestruck ladykiller
a poetic literal: word lengths mod 10 spell the digits. a (1) lovestruck (10→0) ladykiller (10→0) = 100, the loop limit. Likewise Fire is ice makes 3 and Hate is water makes 5.
Build my world up
my world = my world + 1 — the loop counter, incremented once per verse.
If Midnight taking my world, Fire is nothing…
if n mod 3 == 0. taking is a function call, nothing is zero, and Shout/Say/Whisper are all just print — dynamics are for the performance, not the semantics.
Rockstar and the Starship engine are by Dylan Beattie and contributors (source, AGPL-3.0); the engine binaries in wasm/ are the official WebAssembly build, stored gzipped (3.2 MB down from 9.7) and inflated in-browser by a DecompressionStream. This page is part of fizzybuzzy.
